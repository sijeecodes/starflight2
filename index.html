goal

destroy enemies while not getting destroyed.

tips

  • put targets between the green aim triangles for better shots.
  • use "roll" to dodge enemy attacks.
    you won't get hit while rolling.
  • boost forward to evade enemies attacking from sides
    or pass through small enemies.
